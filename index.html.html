Our Story

More Than Just Food — It's Made With Love.

SAFAMU FOOD's was born from a simple but powerful belief — that everyone deserves a delicious, home-cooked meal without the hassle. What started as a passion for cooking in a small kitchen in Utawala, Nairobi, has grown into a beloved food brand trusted by families and food lovers across the city.

Every dish we make is prepared fresh, with carefully selected ingredients and a whole lot of love. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just real, honest food that tastes like it came straight from your mum's kitchen.

Whether you're ordering a quick snack or a full hearty meal, we pour our heart into every plate. Because at SAFAMU FOOD's, feeding you well isn't just what we do — it's who we are.
